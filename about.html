About Us

The Department of Hospitality Management had its inception in the year 2014 and it offers BVoc Hospitality Management. It is a three year degree in Bachelor of Vocations started based on the scheme launched by University Grants Commission (UGC). This course incorporates specific job roles and their National Occupational Standards (NOS) under Tourism & Hospitality Skill Council (THSC) constituted by National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) along with the judicious mix of skills relating to a profession and appropriate content of general education.
